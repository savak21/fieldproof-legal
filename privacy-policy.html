FieldProof — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

FieldProof ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application for capturing cryptographically verified photo evidence. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights over your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. You sign in with your phone number (verified by a one-time SMS code) or with Google. Depending on the method we collect your phone number, or your Google account's name and email address, plus the name you provide. Your name appears in the watermark of photos you capture, establishing your identity as the photographer.

Evidence Metadata. When you take a verified photo, we record:

  • GPS coordinates and reverse-geocoded address at the moment of capture
  • Server-verified timestamp
  • Device model and operating system version
  • A device identifier (used to link your device's public signing key)
  • A SHA-256 cryptographic hash of your photo
  • Project name and optional notes you enter

Device Signing Key. A cryptographic key pair is generated on your device. The private key never leaves your device. The public key is registered in our database so third parties can verify that photos came from a FieldProof device.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your actual photo files — photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Advertising identifiers or device tracking IDs.
  • Contacts, messages, or other apps' data.
  • Continuous background location — location is accessed only during photo capture.
  • Browsing history or behavioral analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and protect your evidence.
  • To embed verified identity (name, GPS, timestamp) into the watermark burned onto each photo.
  • To allow third parties to independently verify the authenticity of photos you share, by checking the cryptographic signature against our device key registry.
  • To provide the offline sync queue so captures saved without network access are synced when connectivity returns.

4. Data Storage and Security

Evidence metadata and account information are stored on Supabase infrastructure (PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS). All data is protected by:

  • TLS encryption in transit
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring you can only access your own data
  • Hardware-backed secure storage for your device's private signing key (Android Keystore / iOS Secure Enclave)

Your photos remain on your device in the app's private storage directory and are not accessible to other apps.

5. Data Sharing

We share data with the following third-party service provider:

Supabase — Database hosting and user authentication infrastructure, including delivery of one-time SMS verification codes through Supabase's SMS provider. Supabase processes account information and evidence metadata on our behalf. Supabase Privacy Policy

Google Sign-In — If you choose to sign in with Google, Google authenticates you and provides us your name and email address. Google Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties for commercial or marketing purposes. We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs.

Verification Lookups. Your device's public key and device ID are publicly readable in our database specifically to enable independent verification. Verifiers can also check whether a photo's cryptographic hash matches a recorded capture; this returns only a yes/no answer. No personal information (name, email, GPS history) is exposed by these lookups.

6. Permissions We Request

  • Camera — Required to capture photos. Accessed only when you actively use the Capture screen.
  • Location (Fine & Coarse) — Required to embed GPS coordinates in evidence watermarks. Read only while the Capture screen is open. We do not request background location.
  • Photo Library (Read) — Required to select photos for the Verify feature. We do not modify or upload photos from your library.
  • Storage — Required to save your captured evidence photos to your device's app storage.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Access. You can view all your evidence photos and metadata within the app.

Deletion of Evidence. You can delete individual photos or entire projects from within the app. Deleted evidence records are permanently removed from our database.

Account Deletion. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Delete Account in the app. This removes your account, all evidence metadata, all projects, and your device key registrations from our servers. This action is irreversible.

Data Export. To request a copy of your data, contact us at savak21@gmail.com.

Correction. To correct your name or email, contact us at savak21@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information and evidence metadata for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Backups are purged on the same schedule.

Photos stored on your device are not affected by account deletion — you must delete them manually from your device if desired.

9. Children's Privacy

FieldProof is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at savak21@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by displaying a notice in the app before the change takes effect. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
